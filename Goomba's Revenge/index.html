Goomba's Revenge

  • High Concept: Get revenge on your goomba bretheren: jump on the shells and hit them back at Mario!
  • Theme: Revenge.
  • Mandated Variety: Input: Left Click
  • Player Experience Goals: "What a cool loading screen".
  • Gameplay: What gameplay?
  • Prototype Goal: To have a cool loading screen.
  • Features: Extreme polish.
  • Setting/Premise: Mario killed your goomba buddy.
  • Story: Mario killed your goomba buddy, so kill Mario.
  • Target Audience: Fucking casuals.
  • Play Time: 20 seconds.
  • Strategies: Since the asteroids all head for the center of the Earth sprite, it is wise to wait until the asteroids bunch up so that you can take out many at once with a single shot. Reloading when there aren't many asteroids on screen is also a good strategy.

  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Really close to Super Mario World.
