Goomba's Revenge

  • High Concept: Avenge your goomba bretheren: jump on the shells and hit them back at Mario!
  • Theme: I used the revenge theme for this week's game.
  • Mandated Variety:
    • Input: Keyboard
    • Randomness: Deterministic
    • Play Style: Competitor
    • Genre: Side-Scroller
  • Player Experience Goals: Players should feel that the game aesthetics are very polished, like it's a real Super Mario World spinoff. The game's randomness shouldn't feel too unfair. Players should feel great when they get a new high score, and also moderately challenged.
  • Gameplay: Press the spacebar to jump. Jump onto a shell to launch it back at Mario. When Mario is hit by a shell, you gain 1 point. If you jump onto or over a shell, a new one spawns when they fly off screen. If you get hit by a shell, the game ends. Aim for the highest score!
  • Prototype Goal: This prototype is evaluating the basic platforming element of jumping combined with a semi-random-speed projectile.
  • Features: The aesthetics are very polished, due to a majority of the game's assets being borrowed from a triple A game. The role reversal of playing as a Goomba instead of Mario is something that no Mario game has done.
  • Setting/Premise: The game takes place in Super Mario World's setting. Mario has killed your goomba friend!
  • Story: There really isn't much of a story here. The player character goomba must get his revenge on Mario by chasing him down and launching shells at him! Mario must pay for what he's done.
  • Target Audience: People who are fans of Mario games or platformers.
  • Play Time: Anywhere from 5 seconds up to an infinite amount of time, due to the infinite scoring nature of the game.
  • Strategies: Correctly timing your jump is the only correct strategy.
  • Prototype Assets:
    • Loading screen text: self-made
    • All Super Mario World assets copyrighted by Nintendo; obtained from Spriter's Resource and The Mushroom Kingdom.net
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: The gameplay is closest to the game I took the art from: Super Mario World. Jumping on shells to launch them is a staple of Mario gameplay.
